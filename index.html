Coding Lessons For Your Kids

Code Haven offers effective and engaging lessons that ignite your childs love for computer science. We offer numerous courses that teach coding while keeping your child entertained.

Write Codes.

Through our innovative program, your child will learn different coding languages from the ground up. They will explore through our immersive world filled with never-ending adventure packed with essential coding knowledge. As they progress, their knowledge in coding will increase along with their love for computers.

This will provide them with a enriched basis of knowledge that will allow them to thrive in a technologically advancing world.

Why Choose Code Haven?

Our passionate team have created a unique method of teaching that will create a brand new expierence with coding education. Our courses use envigorating cartoons that interconnect with coding knowledge and sustainability elements to teach. We are dedicated to bringing Fun, Knowledge and Awareness together and strive to provide your child with a concrete education.
